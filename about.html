About Serena

Serena is a trained hula hooper, dancer, aerial gymnast and flow artist. She started hula hooping 6 years ago and fell in love with the art! Serena loves to spin fire, dragon staff, and multiple hoops. Serena is also professionally trained on pole, aerial hoop, & silks. She has won 1st place at Lindenwood Apollo Night, performed on stage for DJs & Events, as well as been on America's Got Talent 2019! Serena loves teaching hooping, aerial gymnastics, & dance because it is her passion to help others achieve their goals! Three words that describe Serena are expressive, passionate, and positive. Her personal motto is "Positive mind, positive vibes, positive life.
